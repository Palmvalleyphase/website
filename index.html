Project Overview

Discover Palm Valley Phase-3, a premium residential community located in the prime sector of Jalpura, Greater Noida West (Noida Extension). Developed by the renowned Lucky Homes Builders & Developers, this gated community stands as a testament to contemporary design, structural reliability, and luxury living.

Palm Valley Phase-3 features an intelligently planned layout offering both the multi-unit modern Apex Tower apartments and high-end Premium Villas. The villa range includes configurations across Oasis Tower, Sky House Tower, Sky Light Tower (200 Sqyd), Sun Flower Tower (150 Sqyd), and Lotus Tower (174 Sqyd).

Every unit is structured to optimize ventilation, natural light, and space utilization, ensuring an open and breathable atmosphere. With 24/7 smart security, modern lift installations, dedicated parking spaces, and extensive connectivity to key expressways and metro corridors, Palm Valley Phase-3 offers a secure and peaceful haven for families.

Technical Specifications

Structure

Earthquake resistant RCC framed structure built with premium concrete grades and reinforcement steel for complete longevity.

Flooring

Premium double charged Vitrified Tiles in rooms. Anti-skid ceramic tiles in balconies, washrooms, and toilets.

Doors & Windows

Main and internal wooden doors height up to 8 feet. Window panels hardwood framed with premium hardware fittings.

Electrical

Concealed ISI copper wiring in PVC conduits. Ample light & power points with modern modular switches in all areas.

Toilets

Anti-skid floor tiles with designer ceramic wall tiles up to door frame level. Branded sanitaryware and CP fittings.
